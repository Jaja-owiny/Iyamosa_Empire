Meet Our Team

The visionaries behind Iyamossa Empire's success

United by Vision, Driven by Excellence

Born on the fields of JKUAT through our shared passion for American Football, we discovered that our greatest strength lies not just in individual talents, but in our ability to work as a unified team. Each member brings unique expertise, but together we create something extraordinary.

From late-night strategy sessions in dormitories to building a comprehensive service empire, our journey has been defined by collaboration, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

And now at Iyamosa Empire, we turn dreams into impactful brands. We are a full-service creative agency helping schools, businesses, hotels, Inflencers, and organizations to grow their online presence, visibility and reputation through modern and professional solutions.

From web design to digital marketing, branding, and school magazines - we make a difference. Our team is dedicated to delivering results that exceed expectations, one project at a time.
