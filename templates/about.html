About Iyamossa Empire

Our journey from vision to reality

Our Story

Iyamossa Empire was born on the fields of JKUAT in Kenya. A team of visionaries—three students united by American Football—decided to combine their tech, design, and entrepreneurial passions to create something extraordinary.

What started as late-night discussions in dormitories and shared dreams on the football field has evolved into a comprehensive service empire. We've maintained our core values of innovation, teamwork, and excellence while expanding our expertise across multiple industries.

Today, Iyamossa Empire stands as a testament to the power of collaboration, determination, and the belief that with the right team, any vision can become reality.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our growth story

12/2023

The Beginning

Three JKUAT students with a shared passion for American Football and technology decide to combine their skills and start Iyamossa Empire.

5/2024

First Projects

Launched our first digital marketing campaigns and web design projects, establishing our reputation for quality and innovation.

9/2024

Service Expansion

Expanded into photography, graphic design, and automation services, becoming a comprehensive creative and technical solution provider.

4/2025

Full Service Empire

Added furniture sales and construction services, completing our transformation into a full-service empire serving diverse client needs.
